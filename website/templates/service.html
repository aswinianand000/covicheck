Products & Services

Early detection of the new coronavirus is critical to preventing the virus from spreading to others. We have developed a system based on deep transfer learning that uses chest X-ray pictures from patients with COVID-19 and patients who do not have COVID-19 to detect the condition automatically. Our fine-tuned model performs well in the categorization of COVID-19 pneumonia after rapidly training via a relatively limited image collection.
Our belief is that the suggested computer-aided diagnosis process might significantly enhance COVID-19 case diagnosis. The proposed classification model for identifying COVID-19 has a detection accuracy of greater than 70%. Due to its strong overall performance, we feel it is of nature to aid doctors and health professionals in making clinical judgments, according to our research findings.
